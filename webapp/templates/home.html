How It Works

1

Describe or Upload

Write a prompt, paste a reference video URL, or upload your own audio narration.

2

AI Analyzes

Gemini transcribes your audio, extracts characters and scenes, and analyzes the reference video style.

3

4

Powered by Google AI

Gemini AI

Script generation, scene planning, audio transcription with timestamps, character extraction, intent parsing, subtitle translation, and post-creation verification.

Google Imagen

AI image generation with character-consistent visuals. Each scene gets a detailed prompt with matching art style, characters, and locations.

Google Cloud TTS

Professional narration in 30+ languages including Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, and more Indian languages.

Reference Video Matching

Paste a YouTube URL and the AI analyzes its art style, editing approach, color palette, and mood to create similar content.

Audio-Synced Timing

Sentence-level timestamps ensure each image appears exactly when its corresponding audio plays. Frame-by-frame precision.

Multi-Language Subtitles

Optional subtitles in any language. Gemini translates narration to your chosen subtitle language with natural phrasing.
